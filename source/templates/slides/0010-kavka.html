Kavka.be

Youth organisation in Antwerp

Kavka works with volunteers, hosts a co-working space and organises events for young people in Antwerp. This huge organisation wanted to have a website which would guide the visitor through all their activities and side-activities. Together with Spaced, they took care of the workshops, we conceptualized and developed a website which gave an overview of everything the organisation really does.
